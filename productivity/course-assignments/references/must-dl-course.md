## LAMS Activity Structure (per lecture)
Each lecture has 4 sequential activities:
1. **Noticeboard** — announcement text
2. **Self-study** — learning materials + 10-question pre-class quiz (≥70% to pass)
3. **Assessment** — 20 multiple-choice questions (graded)
4. **Gate** — requires manual lecturer approval
