# MUST BSAI Internship Requirements (2026)

Source: Official internship briefing PDFs, MUST Faculty of Innovation Engineering

## Key Requirements for BSAI Students
- **Hours**: 320 hours (40 workdays × 8 hours, weekends NOT counted)
- **Timeline**: June–August 2026
- **Confirmation form deadline**: **July 14, 2026** (earlier than BIT's Aug 4)
- **WeMust course confirmation**: April 29 22:00 – May 2 20:00
- **Report deadline**: September 15, 2026
- **Report language**: English, ≥2000 words
- **Scoring**: Company evaluation form 50% + Internship report 50%
- **No salary allowed**, only allowances (meals, transport)

## Company Verification Mechanism
1. Supervisor contacts company to check student performance (video or site visit)
2. Company supervisor submits stamped evaluation form
3. Report must include work result photos
4. Content must be IT/engineering related (NOT data entry, marketing, HR)
5. Absenteeism >30% = automatic fail
6. False documentation = disciplinary action per student code

## Self-Sourced Internship (找关系企业)
- Allowed: students can find their own internship companies
- Must be IT/engineering related work
- Need a designated "supervisor" who can answer school inquiries and stamp forms
- Must produce real deliverables/photos for the report
- Remote work not explicitly prohibited but not officially sanctioned
