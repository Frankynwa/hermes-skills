## Admission Requirements (verbatim from official page)

### General Requirements
> Master's: "Obtained a bachelor's degree from a recognized institution, or an approved equivalent qualification."
> PhD: "Obtained a bachelor's degree with a proven record of outstanding performance from a recognized institution..."

**⚠️ NO GPA MINIMUM NUMBER STATED.** Just "recognized institution" + "bachelor's degree."

### English Language Requirements
- TOEFL-iBT: 80 (tests before Jan 21, 2026) / 4.5 (from Jan 21, 2026 onwards)
- TOEFL-pBT: 550
- IELTS (Academic): Overall 6.5, all sub-scores 5.5
- Exempt if: first language is English, OR bachelor's from English-medium institution
- "Test at home" option NOT accepted

### Program-Specific
- Some programs may have additional requirements (check per-program pages)

## Key Differences from CityU-DG
| Aspect | HKUST-GZ | CityU-DG |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Program types | Mainly MPhil/PhD (research) | Mainly MSc (taught) |
| Professor discretion | **HIGH** (research degrees) | Moderate (taught degrees) |
| English requirement | IELTS 6.5 / TOEFL 80 | CET-6 425 / IELTS 6.0 |
| Research opportunities | Extensive (cross-campus with HKUST) | Limited (mainly coursework) |
| Campus age | 2022 (4 years) | 2024 (2 years) |
| Degree awarded | HKUST degree | CityU HK degree |

## Strategy for Low-GPA Applicants
1. **MPhil/PhD in AI or Financial Technology** — professor has strong discretion, target these
2. **MSc in Data-Centric AI** — taught program, but still worth trying (no stated GPA minimum)
3. **Contact professors in Information Hub** — AI and Data Science thrust areas
4. **Cross-campus opportunity** — can access HKUST Hong Kong research facilities
5. **Funding** — MPhil/PhD students typically receive stipends; MSc is self-funded
