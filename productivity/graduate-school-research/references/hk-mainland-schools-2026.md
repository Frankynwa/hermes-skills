**FAQ (from official site, accessed July 2026):**
- Entry: "持有获认可的学士学位" — no GPA number stated
- English: CET-6 425 / TOEFL 79 / IELTS 6.0
- 80% faculty from CityU HK, 70% from 35 countries
- Teaching language: English (some Chinese courses in Mandarin)
- Cross-major application: allowed
- Recommendation letters: required
