## Tier 1: Best Options (Realistic + Good Value)

### 🇭🇰 CityU Dongguan (港城东莞) — QS #52
- Programs: MSc AI, MSc CS, MSc Data Science, MSc Business Analytics, + 6 more
- GPA: **No explicit minimum stated on official website**
- English: **CET-6 425 accepted** (unique advantage)
- Tuition: ¥10-16万 total (1-1.5 years)
- Degree: CityU Hong Kong master's certificate
- Post-grad: 2-year IANG visa (Hong Kong work)
- Website: pga.cityu-dg.edu.cn
- Contact: admissions@cityu-dg.edu.cn, 0769-21183030

### 🇮🇹 University of Trento — QS #335
- Programs: MSc Computer Science, MSc AI/Data Science
- GPA: 90/110 Italian ≈ GPA 2.5 — **exact match**
- English: IELTS 6.0
- Tuition: €3,500/year (≈¥2.7万/year)
- Italy's top CS department
- Application: March-July for October start

### 🇧🇪 VUB Brussels — QS ~200
- Programs: MSc Computer Science, MSc AI
- GPA: **No hard cutoff**, holistic review
- English: IELTS 6.5
- Tuition: €1,000-5,000/year (≈¥0.8-4万/year)
- Very welcoming to international students

### 🇳🇴 NTNU Norway — QS #264 — ⚠️ NO LONGER BUDGET OPTION
- Programs: MSc Computer Science, MSc AI
- GPA: C average minimum (≈GPA 2.0)
- English: IELTS 6.5; CET-6 NOT accepted
- Tuition: **NOK 150,000–200,000/year (~¥10-13万/yr)** for non-EU since autumn 2023
- Previously free, policy changed 2023
- Living cost: ~¥10-12万/year (Norway is expensive)
- **Conclusion**: With tuition + living = ¥20-25万/year, no longer competitive vs Italy/Belgium

### 🇧🇪 Ghent University — QS ~150
- Programs: MSc Computer Science
- GPA: Flexible admission, holistic
- English: IELTS 6.5
- Tuition: €3,900/year (≈¥3万/year)
