# School-by-School GPA Policy Reference

Last updated: 2026-07-01
Based on research for MUST BSAI student with GPA ~2.5/4.0

## 🇭🇰 Hong Kong & Mainland Campuses

### CityU Dongguan (香港城市大学东莞) — BEST BET
- **Opened**: 2024 (first graduation 2026, actively recruiting)
- **Programs (10 MSc)**: AI, Computer Science, Data Science, Business & Data Analytics, Business Info Systems, Electronic Engineering, Engineering Management, Materials Eng, Biomedical Eng, Innovation & Entrepreneurship
- **GPA floor**: **NO MINIMUM STATED** (official FAQ: "持有获认可的学士学位")
- **English**: **CET-6 425 accepted** (or IELTS 6.0 / TOEFL 79)
- **Degree**: CityU Hong Kong degree (same as HK campus)
- **Cross-major**: Allowed
- **Post-grad**: 2-year Hong Kong IANG work visa
- **Professor discretion**: Moderate (mainly taught MSc, no MPhil yet confirmed)
- **Contact**: admissions@cityu-dg.edu.cn, 0769-21183030
- **Tuition**: ¥150,000/yr × 2yr = ¥300,000 total (申请费免费)
- **Application deadline**: July 31, 2026 (rolling admission)
- **⚠️ No "internal recommendation" mechanism** — school explicitly warns against intermediaries claiming otherwise
- **Language**: English primary
- **Portal**: pga.cityu-dg.edu.cn (NOT main site which is JS-only)
- See `references/cityu-dg-official.md` for full details

### HKUST Guangzhou (港科大广州) — WORTH TRYING
- **Opened**: 2022 (4 years, still growing)
- **Programs (all MPhil/PhD unless noted)**:
  - Information Hub: AI, Data Science & Analytics, Data-Centric AI (**MSc**), Computational Media & Arts, IoT
  - Society Hub: **Financial Technology** (perfect for quant!), Carbon Neutrality, Urban Governance
  - Systems Hub: Robotics, Smart Manufacturing (MPhil/PhD + **MSc**), Intelligent Transportation
  - Function Hub: Advanced Materials, Microelectronics, etc.
- **GPA floor**: **NO MINIMUM STATED** (official: "bachelor's degree from recognized institution")
- **English**: IELTS 6.5 (all 5.5) / TOEFL 80
- **Degree**: HKUST Hong Kong degree
- **Professor discretion**: HIGH — research degrees are professor-driven
- **Cross-campus**: Can access HKUST HK research facilities
- **Funding**: MPhil/PhD typically funded (PGS ¥10,000/month); MSc self-funded. Red Bird MPhil scholarship ¥120,000/year. Tuition with PGS: ¥40,000/yr (rising to ¥60k for 2027/28+). Self-funded: ¥150,000/yr. CET-6 NOT accepted.
- **Portal**: fytgs.hkust-gz.edu.cn
- See `references/hkust-gz-official.md` for full details

### CUHK Shenzhen (港中文深圳) — HARDER
- **Opened**: 2014 (well-established, very competitive now)
- **GPA floor**: ~3.3 effective (popular programs)
- **Professor discretion**: MODERATE — formalized process
- **Note**: Now very popular, GPA enforcement stricter

### CityU HK Main Campus
- **Taught MSc**: Hard cutoff ~3.0, auto-filtered
- **MPhil/PhD**: Professor nomination system, exception policy exists
- **Contact**: wcleong@must.edu.mo, wmung@must.edu.mo (MUST liaison)

### HKUST HK Main Campus
- **MPhil/PhD**: Professor submits nomination → dept → school → grad school
- **80% of PhD decision = supervisor preference** (unwritten rule)
- **Scholarship (HKPFS)**: Hard GPA cutoff, cannot override

### CUHK HK Main Campus
- **Explicit policy**: "candidates not meeting minimum may be admitted on recommendation of Graduate Division with Graduate Council approval"
- **Most explicit exception language among HK schools**

## 🇦🇺 Australia

### Monash University — BEST BET
- **Minimum**: 60% (≈GPA 2.4/4.0) — YOU MEET THIS
- **Flexibility**: Most flexible among Go8, considers "equivalent qualifications and experience"
- **Conditional offers**: Yes, regularly
- **MPhil**: Supervisor discretion paramount
- **Programs**: Master of AI, Master of Data Science, Master of IT

### UNSW — GOOD CHANCE
- **Minimum**: 65% (≈GPA 2.6/4.0) for domestic, 75% for international
- **Flexibility**: Moderate holistic review
- **MPhil**: More flexible with supervisor support

### University of Melbourne
- **Minimum**: 65% (≈GPA 2.6-2.8)
- **Flexibility**: Most competitive Australian school, strict cutoffs
- **3-year stream**: Possible pathway for borderline students

### ANU
- **Minimum**: 5.0/7.0 (≈GPA 2.8)
- **Flexibility**: Considers research experience and publications

## 🇸🇬 Singapore

### NUS — VERY DIFFICULT
- **Minimum**: B+ (≈3.3/4.0), CS/AI typically 3.5+
- **Professor discretion**: Very limited even for research degrees
- **Verdict**: Skip unless extraordinary profile

### NTU — DIFFICULT
- **Minimum**: B (3.0/4.0), competitive programs B+
- **Professor discretion**: Limited

### SMU — WORTH TRYING
- **MITB (IT in Business)**: FinTech/AI tracks
- **More holistic**: Values work experience, interviews
- **Professor discretion**: Moderate

### SUTD — WORTH TRYING
- **Newer school**, more open to non-traditional applicants
- **Holistic review**: Design thinking, innovation experience

## 🇨🇦 Canada

### UdeM (蒙特利尔大学) — MOST PROMISING TOP-TIER
- **MILAI affiliation** — world-class AI research
- **Professor direct recruitment model** — supervisor wants you = admission
- **"Directed studies" model**: Professor's willingness to supervise is near-prerequisite
- **GPA floor**: 3.0 stated, but professor advocacy works

### McMaster — MORE REALISTIC
- **"Special Admission" with professor recommendation** — commonly used
- **Less competitive** than UofT/UBC/Waterloo

### UAlberta — POSSIBLE
- **Amii (AI institute)** — top RL/ML research
- **"Provisional admission"** for below-minimum students with supervisor support

### University of Manitoba — ACCESSIBLE
- Strong AI group, genuinely flexible admission
- Less competitive than UofT/UBC/Waterloo

### Concordia University — ACCESSIBLE
- Montreal, applied CS, industry-oriented

### UofT / UBC / Waterloo — SKIP
- University-wide SGS floor ~3.0, effectively enforced
- CS departments admit A- (3.7+) averages

## 🇪🇺 Europe

### Aalto University (Finland) — BEST BET
- **Explicitly values portfolio, hackathon wins, open-source contributions**
- "We evaluate your potential, not just past grades"
- Tuition: €15k/year (scholarships 50-100% available)
- **Most holistic** among European options

### KU Leuven (Belgium) — GOOD CHANCE
- Holistic review, values motivation + work experience
- Tuition: €6.6k/year (non-EU)
- Research group positions available

### NTNU (Norway) — ⚠️ NO LONGER FREE
- **Tuition for non-EU**: NOK 150,000–200,000/year (~€13,000–17,500) since autumn 2023
- Previously free, policy changed 2023. No longer a budget option.
- IELTS 6.5 required, CET-6 NOT accepted
- Strong AI research groups, but now expensive for non-EU students

### TU Delft (Netherlands) — PRE-MASTER PATHWAY
- Pre-Master bridging program → guaranteed MSc admission
- Tuition: ~€18k/year

### University of Trento (Italy)
- Less competitive than PoliMi, good AI/ML groups
- Income-based tuition, can be near €0

### ETH/EPFL/TU Munich/RWTH/KTH — SKIP
- GPA 3.5+ required, no realistic path with 2.5
