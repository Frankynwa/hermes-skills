# Verified Data from Official Sources

## NTNU (Norway) — Verified 2026-07-01

**Source:** https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/tuition-fees (accessed via curl)

### Tuition Fees for Non-EU Students (2026/27)
- **Category 1** (Humanities, social sciences, business): NOK 176,300/year
- **Category 2** (Natural sciences, technology, CS): **NOK 205,600/year** (~¥13.5万 RMB)
- **Category 3** (Medicine, dentistry): NOK 528,650/year

### Exemptions (do NOT pay tuition)
- EU/EEA citizens or Swiss citizens
- Exchange students from NTNU partner universities
- PhD/doctoral students
- Certain scholarship holders (StAR, NORPART, NORHED, Fulbright, etc.)

### Key Facts
- NTNU does NOT offer scholarships that cover tuition fees
- **Previously claimed "NTNU is free for all international students" — THIS IS WRONG.** NTNU started charging non-EU tuition around 2023.
